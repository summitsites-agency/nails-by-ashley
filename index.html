The Studio

Ten years of
tiny masterpieces.

Ashley Rivera is a licensed nail artist who treats the nail like a canvas and the appointment like a ritual. Trained in structured gel and sculptured acrylic, she's spent a decade building sets that hold up on set, on stage and on the school run.

The studio is one chair, by appointment only — no rush, no assembly line. Just you, the color wall, and however long it takes to get it right.
